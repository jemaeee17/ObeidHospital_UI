About Obeid Specialized Hospital

Obeid Specialized Hospital is one of the oldest private healthcare institutions in Riyadh, offering a fully integrated and modern facility built to keep pace with the evolving standards of healthcare in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. The hospital comprises 220 rooms, including private and shared rooms, suites, and a royal suite. With over 100 doctors and 200 nurses, the hospital is equipped with the latest advanced diagnostic technologies and features specialized departments such as open-heart surgery, cardiac catheterization, and oncology. We attract highly skilled medical professionals across various specialties, proudly contributing to the advancement of healthcare in our Kingdom and supporting the goals of Vision 2030. Our commitment is to provide exceptional healthcare services that align with the expectations of our patients and their families—delivered with compassion, dignity, and respect. We place the needs of our patients above all and work collaboratively with professionalism and integrity to ensure the highest quality outcomes. To enhance efficiency, we've adopted a modern electronic information system that streamlines service delivery, reduces paperwork, and ensures all medical records are securely stored and easily accessible to both patients and healthcare providers.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Obeid Specialized Hospital is committed to delivering high-quality medical services by fostering a safe and comfortable environment for all patients. The hospital is staffed by skilled and experienced healthcare professionals who uphold the highest standards of ethical conduct. With a strong focus on innovation, the facility is equipped with advanced medical technologies to ensure accurate diagnosis and effective treatment.

Our Vision

Obeid Specialized Hospital envisions becoming a center of excellence in healthcare, recognized for upholding exceptional standards in patient care. The hospital continuously strives for innovation, aiming to stay at the forefront of medical advancements and adopt best practices that enhance healthcare delivery.
